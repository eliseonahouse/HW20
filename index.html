Meet Your New Travel Companion

Lodger is a unique travel app that allows you to plan for your next vacation by finding areas of interest in the city and shop for lodging accommodations nearby.

Research

Affinity Diagram

I conducted qualitative interviews regarding travel preferences and planning with five interviewees. Responses were organized into an affinity diagram allowing common themes and needs to emerge.

Empathy Map

Using responses from qualitative interviews, I created an empathy map in order to identify user motivations, needs, and pain points.

User Persona

Having conducted this research, I was able to formulate a user persona.

Problem Statement

It is hard to choose where to stay in a new city when all you see is a list of hotels arranged by price. Even seeing locations mapped out on a map is less than helpful if you know nothing about your destination city. That is why I would like to create an app that provides the convenience of being able to compare lodging options by price on a map with the added benefit of providing important information about the city through color-coded zones, filterable by desired activity (local charm, nightlife, natural attractions, cultural interests, family fun, etc.).
